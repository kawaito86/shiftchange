Welcome to the Shift Management System!

How to Use the Website After Login:

  • Dashboard Overview: After logging in, you will see a two-week calendar with each day displayed in a box.
  • Adding a Shift Request: Click on a day to request a shift change by entering your original and requested shifts.
  • Viewing Shift Requests: Each day's box will display all shift requests made for that date.
  • Managing Your Shift Requests: Your requests will have a delete button, allowing you to remove them if needed.
  • Contacting Other Users: Click on a shift request to offer a shift and contact the user via WhatsApp.
  • Logging Out: Click the "Logout" button at the top-right corner to end your session.

Enjoy Seamless Shift Management!

If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to reach out for assistance.
